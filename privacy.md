# Privacy Policy — Coach Alter Ego

Last updated: March 23, 2026

## Overview
Coach Alter Ego ("we", "our", "the app") is an AI-powered 
endurance sports coaching platform developed by Alter Ego 
(Mexico City, Mexico).

## Data We Collect
We collect fitness and health data from connected devices 
(Garmin, Apple Watch, Coros) including: heart rate, HRV, 
sleep data, training load, Body Battery, and activity history.
This data is used exclusively to generate personalized 
training plans for the athlete.

## How We Use Your Data
- To generate AI-powered training recommendations.
- To predict race performance and adjust training plans.
- Data is NEVER sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.

## Data Storage
Athlete data is stored securely and is only accessible 
by the athlete who owns it.
